Integrate your WordPress restaurant website with Nexus POS management system.

== Description ==

Nexus POS Integration allows you to connect your WordPress website with the Nexus POS restaurant management system. Your customers can browse your menu and place orders directly on your website, while all orders sync automatically to your Nexus POS dashboard.

**Features:**

* Display your full restaurant menu
* Accept online orders
* Floating "Order Now" button
* Automatic order sync to Nexus POS
* Dark and light theme support
* Gutenberg block support
* Easy shortcode integration

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to `/wp-content/plugins/nexus-pos`
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3. Go to Nexus POS in the admin menu
4. Enter your API Key and Restaurant Slug
5. Save settings
